Farming with Purpose, Feeding Our Community

Located right here in Uganda, Matovu Farms was built on a simple premise: everyone deserves access to high-quality, ethically raised, and affordable meats. With over a decade of experience, we take pride in our sustainable farming practices.

  • Ethical Practices: Our livestock is raised in clean, spacious environments.
  • 100% Natural Feed: We avoid harmful growth hormones.
  • Community First: We partner with local farmers and support the Ugandan agricultural ecosystem.
